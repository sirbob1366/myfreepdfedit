About MyFreePDFEdit

Free PDF tools that respect your privacy.

MyFreePDFEdit is a free toolkit for working with PDF files — merge, split, compress, rotate, sign, annotate, edit and convert — all from a single website. There's no signup, no watermark, and no file-count limits.

Privacy by design

Most of our tools run entirely in your browser. When you merge, split, rotate, compress, sign or edit a PDF, the file is processed on your own device using open-source libraries like pdf-lib and pdf.js. Your document is never uploaded to a server for these tasks. A small number of conversion tools may require server processing — when that's the case, we say so on the tool's page.

What we believe

  • Free should mean free. No paywalls hiding basic features, no surprise charges.
  • Your files are yours. We don't want them, store them, or sell them.
  • It should just work. On any modern browser, on desktop or mobile, with no install.

How we keep the lights on

The site is supported by unobtrusive advertising. That lets us keep every tool free for everyone. See our Privacy Policy for details on advertising and cookies.
