Before you write

Many common questions are answered on our FAQ. If you're reporting a problem with a specific tool, including the following helps us help you faster:

  • Which tool or page you were using.
  • Your browser and device (e.g. Chrome on Windows, Safari on iPhone).
  • What you expected to happen, and what actually happened.

Please don't email us your actual PDF files unless we ask — in most cases a description is enough, and your files are processed privately on your own device.
