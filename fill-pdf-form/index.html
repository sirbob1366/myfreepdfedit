About filling PDF forms

Open any fillable PDF — tax forms, applications, contracts — and your browser renders the document with live inputs over every field. Fill in text, tick checkboxes, choose radio options and dropdown values, then download. Leave Flatten on export enabled to bake your answers permanently into the page (recipients can't alter them), or turn it off to keep the form editable. Filling happens entirely in your browser via the open-source pdf-lib library — your data never leaves your device.
