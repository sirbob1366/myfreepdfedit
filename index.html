Frequently asked questions

Is MyFreePDFEdit really free?

Yes. Every tool is free with no signup, no watermark, and no file count limits. We support the site through unobtrusive ads.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

For most tools — merge, split, compress, sign, annotate, rotate, OCR, protect — your files are processed entirely in your browser and never leave your device. A small number of conversion tools may use server processing; we always say so on the tool page.

Is there a file size limit?

There's no hard limit, but very large files (over 500 MB) may be slow depending on your device. For best results we recommend files under 200 MB.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The site is fully responsive and works on iPhone, iPad and Android devices.

Do I need to install anything?

No. MyFreePDFEdit runs in any modern browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge. Nothing to install or download.
