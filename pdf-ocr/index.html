OCR PDF

Turn scanned PDFs into searchable, copyable documents — free, unlimited, and entirely on your device.

Heads up: OCR is compute-heavy and runs on your processor. A long document can take several minutes — keep this tab open and plugged in. The upside: there's no page cap and nothing is uploaded.

Free, unlimited, on-device OCR

Most online OCR tools cap you at a few pages or hide results behind a paywall — because they pay for servers to process your files. This one is different: it runs the open-source Tesseract engine as WebAssembly inside your browser, so there's nothing for us to meter. Every page of your scan is recognized on your own device and rebuilt as a searchable PDF: the original page image stays pixel-identical on top, with an invisible text layer underneath that you can search, select and copy. Eleven recognition languages are available, including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic and Hindi.
