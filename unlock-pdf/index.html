Unlock PDF

Know the password but tired of typing it? Remove protection from your own PDF — in your browser.

We don't crack passwords. This tool removes protection only when you provide the correct password — for example, your own files or documents you're authorized to use. If you've lost the password, no honest browser tool can help; contact whoever issued the document.

About removing PDF passwords

Bank statements, payslips and invoices often arrive password-protected, which gets tedious if you archive them. This tool decrypts your PDF once — with the password you already know — and saves a permanently unlocked copy. It uses the open-source qpdf engine compiled to WebAssembly, running entirely in your browser: your document and password are never uploaded anywhere.
